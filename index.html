About Us

Aquarius Care Home is a Dual Registered dementia nursing home offering 20 single rooms, some of which are en-suite rooms, to the elderly requiring day-to-day living assistance.

The substantial bungalow has superb outside and communal living areas which have been recently refurbished, and the home prides itself in having a 5-star food and hygiene rating.

Gold Standards awarded. Investors in People awarded in 2018. There is a registered manager in place, with over 25 years of experience in the care management sector, who is also a qualified nurse.

Our Experience

Our long established experience in operating care and nursing homes allows us to offer an extremely high level of quality and service incorporating very high standards with excellent care practices. Our professional care team is dedicated to maintaining excellent care standards directed by our highly qualified Registered Manager.

Aquarius offers a safe and comfortable home for the elderly with friendly, professional, helpful and highly qualified staff with a warm and approachable atmosphere for those adults needing assistance for their day-to-day needs.
